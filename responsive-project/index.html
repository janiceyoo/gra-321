Charlie’s Café, one of Sun Devil Dining’s local restaurant partners, brings the taste of Europe to ASU! Proudly serving Lavazza Italian espresso and featuring fresh European sandwiches and baked goods. Come experience the difference! With three locations on campus, there is always a Charlie’s Cafe near! Charlie’s Café is located at the Hayden Library, Bio Design B Building and the College of Design.

“The people here are so friendly and are very kind. When I have free time, I like to come here and enjoy my latte.” -Harry Styles

World renown singer/songwriter/actor, Harry Styles, is a loyal Charlie’s customer! You can find him hanging out at the cafe or coming to get his coffee before heading to the studio. If you are lucky, maybe you can even say hi. His go-to orders are a Vanilla Latte with a shot of caramel creamer or a black coffee.
